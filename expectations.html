What is a Reiki session like?

Reiki uses the hands to move the energy, sometimes placing them on a person or just having them above areas of the body. Depending on the progression of the in person session there will be several ways in which the hands are used. There are some ways that may differ depending on the practitioner and what they have learned from their own experience. There is no concrete way because sometimes the practitioner will just follow their intuition from signals they feel from the person receiving the reiki. The practitioner usually can feel what may need to be done.

With that being said, there are certain positions that are learned that are a basic routine that can be used. Again each individual session will vary depending on the unique circumstances.
