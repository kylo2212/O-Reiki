History of Oden Reiki

Oden reiki is a newly learned way to work with the energy of the body using new energies to work through for a new reiki experience. Lauralei Bradley channeled the ways of these new techniques and became attuned to them through many days of meditation over several years with Oden. She has taught others these special techniques and passed on the attunements in order to spread these new energy healing techniques. Attunement in reiki terms means becoming more aware and receptive to the energies. This is done in levels. Oden reiki has many levels that are taught and attuned. Please visit Oden Reiki to learn about these energies in more detail.
